How to Multiply Numbers

Here you will find instruction about using the multiplication feature of JPS Calculator app

To successfully multiply numbers, you must know what is Multiplication

  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the multiply (x) button
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (r) button.

The result from the multiplication is displayed.

You want to multiply 2 and 3. The equation will look like: 2x3=6. You enter 2. Press multiply. Enter 3. Press equals. The result 6 appears on the screen.
