How to Divide Numbers

Here you will find instruction about using the Division feature of JPS Calculator app

To successfully divide numbers, you must know what is Division

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the divide (รท) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (r) button.

The result from the division is displayed.

You want to divide 6 by 2. The equation will look like: 6รท2=3. You enter 6. Press divide. Enter 3. Press equals. The result 3 appears on the screen.
