How To Sum up Numbers

Here you will find instruction about using the addition feature of JPS Calculator app

To successfully sum up numbers, you must know what is addition.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the plus (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (r) button.

The result from the addition is displayed.

You want to subtract 3 and 2. The equation will look like: 3+2=5. You enter 3. Press minus. Enter 2. Press equals. The result 5 appears on the screen.
